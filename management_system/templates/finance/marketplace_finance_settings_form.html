What This Controls

Phase 2 prepares the accounting map so Phase 3 can post marketplace orders safely.

  • The sales journal will receive marketplace journal entries.
  • The receivable account is debited when an order is posted before cash settlement.
  • The revenue account is credited for the sale value.
  • The tax account is optional for separate tax handling later.
